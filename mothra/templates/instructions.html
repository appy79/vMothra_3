Instructions

How to play :

1. Register yourself first by clicking 'Join Hunt' at homepage.

2. Login by clicking 'Take Flight' at homepage and use your roll number and password given at the time of registration.

3. You start as a young Mothra with level 'born'.

4. Click on 'Go Hunting' to attempt the next challenge.

5. You are given a puzzle. You have to solve it to find the answer.

6. Write the answer and its explanation and submit.

7. If your submission is accepted then you are upgraded to the next level and can attempt a new challenge.

8. The number of attempts left for the particular stage can now be seen on the particular challenge's page.

Tip: Keep and eye out for announcements and notifications.

Submissions :

1. There are two parts of a submission. Answer(Case-sensitive) and Explanation(Non Case-sensitive).

2. When you submit there are 2 parts in which your answer is checked which are Answer Check and Explanation Check.

3. The answer check is handled automatically and if the answer is found to be incorrect then the explanation will not be checked.

4. If the answer is correct then the explanation part is sent to the admin for review.

5. If the admin finds the explanation satisfactory then you will be upgraded to the next level. Otherwise you will get a rejected submission notification.

6. You can expect the review of your submission within 1 hour of your submission. But for the submission given between 12AM to 6AM, the review will be done after 6 AM only.
